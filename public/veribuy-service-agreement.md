## 2. The Service

Subject to the terms of this Agreement and payment of all applicable fees, VeriBuy will provide Customer with access to the Service during the applicable subscription term. - market valuation estimates informed by third-party and publicly available data;
- vehicle history information;

- a web-based dashboard to manage inspections, vehicles, users, and Reports.
